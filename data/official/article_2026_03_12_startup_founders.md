Official page brief based on a March 12, 2026 Youth Affairs Agency news item.

An open meeting was held at the Presidential School in Jizzakh with students and young startup founders. Alisher Sa'dullayev, as Director of the Youth Affairs Agency, reviewed youth projects and listened to startup ideas presented by participants.

The discussion emphasized moving startup ideas toward global markets and helping young founders attract serious investors. Representatives of the "Yoshlar Ventures" Fund also joined the session and gave practical guidance on refining projects and building stronger ventures.

The event also highlighted recent state decisions aimed at developing the startup ecosystem in Uzbekistan and broadening support for young entrepreneurs. This source is useful for questions about startup support, innovation, investor readiness, and practical encouragement for young founders.
