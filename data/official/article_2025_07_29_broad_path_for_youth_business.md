Official page brief based on a July 29, 2025 Youth Affairs Agency news item.

Open dialogues with young entrepreneurs were held in the Republic of Karakalpakstan and Bukhara Region as part of a broader regional support system for youth entrepreneurship and employment.

The meetings focused on shaping and financing business ideas through concrete mechanisms linked to Aloqabank, the Youth Entrepreneurship Development Fund, the Yoshlar Ventures venture fund, and other financing channels. The model was planned for step-by-step rollout across all regions.
