Official page brief based on a February 15, 2026 Youth Affairs Agency news item.

"El-yurt Umidi" scholarship holders visited the Youth Affairs Agency and were introduced to major youth projects. During the meeting, Alisher Sa'dullayev spoke with the students about how they should represent Uzbekistan abroad not only as students, but also as cultural ambassadors.

The meeting connected international education with public service, reputation, and contribution back to the country. At the close of the dialogue, participants received the book "Venture Mindset" along with premium access to the "Ibrat Academy" and "Mutolaa" projects.

This source is useful for questions about global education, studying abroad, cultural representation, books, language learning, and how Alisher frames international experience in relation to Uzbekistan's development.
