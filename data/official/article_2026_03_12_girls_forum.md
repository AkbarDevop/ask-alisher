Official page brief based on a March 12, 2026 Youth Affairs Agency news item.

The "Girls' Forum" was held at the Youth Creativity Palace in Tashkent to strengthen the role of young women in society, support professional development, and open more space for creative and leadership potential.

The event brought together nearly one thousand active girls, students, and young professionals from different regions. It was opened by Dilnozakhon Kattakhanova, First Deputy Director of the Youth Affairs Agency, and included direct conversations with experienced professionals from multiple fields.

The forum introduced new career-oriented courses under the "Girls' Academy" and presented the "Girls' Voice" Club. This source is useful for questions about women's leadership, youth opportunities for girls, professional development tracks, and the Agency's gender-equality programming.
