Official page brief based on a February 14, 2025 presidential meeting with youth representatives.

The meeting covered a broad package of new opportunities for young people: a new graduate employment system supported by the National Bank, preferential loans for youth-led small businesses and training centers, creative industry parks in the regions, and more support for language learning and modern skills.

It also announced major education and mobility measures, including the "Bright Future" study-abroad project for talented youth from socially vulnerable families, wider support through the El-Yurt Umidi Foundation, and housing support for internationally educated young scientists.
