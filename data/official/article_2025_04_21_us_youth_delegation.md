Official page brief based on an April 21, 2025 Youth Affairs Agency news item.

During a visit to the United States, the Uzbekistan youth delegation met with leading companies and universities in Silicon Valley to build practical links for youth development in Uzbekistan.

The visit included meetings with 500 Global, Silkroad Innovation Hub, Stanford scholar Ilya Strebulaev, and Plug and Play. These conversations focused on venture capital, startup ecosystems, startup championships for schoolchildren, global exposure, and ties between Yoshlar Ventures and international partners.
