Official page brief based on a January 29, 2026 Youth Affairs Agency news item.

Launched by the Youth Affairs Agency together with Youth Ventures, the "StartUp Ambassadors" project aims to open startup clubs across Uzbekistan and bring young people into the startup ecosystem earlier.

The project gives participants a first practical entry point: they can develop ideas, find like-minded peers, and build local networks. Startup clubs were announced across all 14 regions of the country, signaling a more distributed approach to startup culture rather than concentrating everything in Tashkent.

This source is useful for questions about regional startup clubs, how young people are introduced to entrepreneurship ecosystems, and the effort to spread startup culture across the country.
