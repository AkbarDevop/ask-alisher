Official page brief based on a September 29, 2025 Youth Affairs Agency statement.

At the High-Level Session of the UN General Assembly marking the 30th anniversary of the World Programme of Action for Youth, Alisher Saidullaev presented Uzbekistan's youth-policy position around three linked priorities: education, entrepreneurship, and employment.

In the speech, he argued that investment in young people determines the future and pointed to the country's practical emphasis on skills for the era of artificial intelligence, youth job creation, and large-scale financial support for youth entrepreneurship. He also connected these goals to the "World Youth Movement for Peace" initiative.
