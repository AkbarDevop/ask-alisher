Official page brief based on a March 5, 2026 Youth Affairs Agency news item.

As part of the Republican Commission's study visit to Andijan Region, an open dialogue was held to support the initiatives of youth leaders and improve the effectiveness of the youth engagement system in mahallas.

With Alisher Sa'dullayev participating directly, the discussion focused on practical issues in local youth work: employment, entrepreneurship, and identifying talented young people in communities. The session was framed as a working meeting for concrete proposals and future tasks, not just a ceremonial event.
