Official page brief based on a March 12, 2026 Youth Affairs Agency news item.

Members of the Republican Commission continued regional work in Jizzakh to improve the system of working with youth in mahallas. In this setting, Alisher Sa'dullayev took part in an open dialogue with youth leaders from local communities.

The meeting focused on practical proposals around youth employment, entrepreneurship, support for talented young people, and raising the effectiveness of local youth work. Youth leaders shared what they are seeing on the ground and what kinds of support would help them serve young people better.

At the end of the event, 20 youth leaders received special vouchers connected to projects such as "Ibrat farzandlari," "Ustoz AI," "UzChess," and "Mutolaa." The source is useful for questions about mahalla-based youth policy, practical support in regions, foreign language learning, reading culture, modern skills, and chess development.
