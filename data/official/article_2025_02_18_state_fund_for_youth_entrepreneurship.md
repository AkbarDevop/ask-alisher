Official page brief based on a February 18, 2025 Youth Affairs Agency news item.

Under Resolution PQ-60, the State Target Fund for the Development of Youth Entrepreneurship was established under the Youth Affairs Agency on the basis of the Young Entrepreneurs Support Fund.

The fund was planned to grow to the equivalent of 100 million US dollars and was given a concrete employment target: to engage at least 50,000 young people in entrepreneurship. The same package also introduced a 10 million US dollar venture mechanism to finance promising youth startup projects.
