Official page brief based on a March 27, 2025 presidential presentation on support for talented youth.

The presentation focused on building a more systematic path for gifted students: an international school for top 8th grade graduates, an Ivy League preparation program, intensive training through the Renaissance educational center, and stronger post-study support for talented youth educated abroad.

It also proposed a "Community of the country's golden fund" to unite gifted young people on a single platform and to connect them with benefits, grants, startup programs, and long-term career opportunities. The President also instructed that opportunities through El-Yurt Umidi should be expanded further.
