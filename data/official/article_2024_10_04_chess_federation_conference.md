Official page brief based on an October 4, 2024 Youth Affairs Agency news item.

An extraordinary meeting of the Uzbekistan Chess Federation Conference was held in Tashkent to approve the federation's new charter and elect new leadership.

At the meeting, Alisher Zafarovich Sa'dullayev, serving as Director of the Youth Affairs Agency, was elected First Vice President of the Uzbekistan Chess Federation. Prime Minister Abdulla Aripov was elected President of the federation.
