Official page brief based on an October 4, 2024 Youth Affairs Agency news item.

At the 4th World Conference on Creative Economy in Tashkent, Alisher Sa'dullayev presented projects being implemented in Uzbekistan in state youth policy, education, vocational training, and the meaningful organization of youth leisure time.

The event brought together more than 2,000 participants from over 80 countries and focused on the future of the creative economy, new technologies, and the impact of artificial intelligence on this sector.
