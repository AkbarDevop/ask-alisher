So'ngi haftalarda Stenforddagi eng tajribali professorlardan biri Uilliam Barnett (William P. Barnett)ning darslaridan postlar yozdim. Ularni quyidagi havolalar orqali o'qishingiz mumkin:

- "Discovering Strategy" (1-qism) (https://t.me/stanford_kundaligi/330)
- "Building Advantages" (2-qism (https://t.me/stanford_kundaligi/332))
- "Building Advantages" bobidan asosiy gʻoyalar (3-qism (https://t.me/stanford_kundaligi/333))
- "Daholik manbayi " (4-qism; 1/2 (https://t.me/stanford_kundaligi/340))
- "Daholik manbayi" (4-qism; 2/2 (https://t.me/stanford_kundaligi/341))
- "Kompaniyani kengaytirishda qanday omillar muhim?" (5-qism (https://t.me/stanford_kundaligi/342))
- "Leading Organizational Change" (6-qism (https://t.me/stanford_kundaligi/344))
- "Leading by Design" (7-qism (https://t.me/stanford_kundaligi/345))

Keyingi postlarimda, Stenforddagi yana bir eng tajribali va o'zining "Venchur Zehniyati" ("The Venture Mindset") nomli kitobi bilan mashhur bo'lgan professor, Ilya Strebulaevning darslari haqidagi postlar bilan ulashib boraman. Fikrlaringiz hamda qo’llovingiz bu jarayonda men uchun juda muhim.

Ilya Strebulaev haqida qisqa ma'lumot:

Ilya Strebulaev Stanford’ning Venture Capital Initiative dasturiga asos solgan va uning direktoridir. U 70 dan ortiq ilmiy maqola va tadqiqotlar muallifi bo‘lib, AQSh va Yevropadagi eng yirik venchur fondlari hamda startaplarga maslahatchi sifatida ham faoliyat olib boradi. Uning ishlari Harvard Business Review, The New York Times, Wall Street Journal va Financial Times kabi nufuzli nashrlarda chop etilgan.
