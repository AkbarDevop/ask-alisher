Alisher Sadullaev, the Chairman of Youth Union (YU) of Uzbekistan briefly laid out the organization's goals while making bold statements on the new structure as well as the mission of the YU.

- No more memberships, so every young person in the country may feel belonged to the organization.
- YU will become more of a 'facilitator' and a supporter of youth projects throughout the country.

I'm sure many more interesting and efficient reforms within the organization are underway.
