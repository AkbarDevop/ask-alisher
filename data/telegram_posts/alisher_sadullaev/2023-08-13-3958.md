⚡️Jamoamizning qat’iyatli va matonatli vakillari

Yodingizda bo‘lsa Surxondaryo (https://t.me/alisher_sadullaev/3028) va Andijonga (https://t.me/alisher_sadullaev/3851) xizmat safarimda Jumageldi aka va Javohir akalar bilan suhbatlashib, ularning kelajakka bo’lgan ishonchi yuksakligi, harakatchanligi haqida yozgandim.
