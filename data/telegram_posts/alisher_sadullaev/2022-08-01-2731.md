Bugun, 1-avgust kuni Butunjahon shaxmat olimpiadasining 4-turi start oladi

Hindistonning Chennay shahrida o‘tayotgan Butunjahon shaxmat olimpiadasining 4-turida erkaklar terma jamoamiz AQSH terma jamoasiga qarshi, ayollar terma jamoamiz esa Dominikan Respublikasi vakillariga qarshi dona suradilar.

4-tur Toshkent vaqti bilan soat 14:30 da start oladi. Vakillarimizning o‘yinlarini onlayn kuzatib borish uchun havola: erkaklar (https://chess24.com/en/watch/live-tournaments/olympiad-chennai-2022-open) / ayollar (https://chess24.com/en/watch/live-tournaments/olympiad-chennai-2022-women). Shaxmat bo‘yicha terma jamoamiz vakillariga bugungi bahslarda omad va g‘alaba tilaymiz!
